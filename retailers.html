    • Audio Analogue

      Our products can boast of the "Made in Italy" brand because they are designed and handmade in Italy. The know-how acquired over the years and the careful choice of local suppliers allows us to keep constant the quality of our products and to be able to keep under control all the steps of production to ensure an excellent final result...

    • Airtech

      Airtech is a brand created by Claudio Bertini (co-founder of Audio Analogue) in 2013 and is part of the portfolio of brands related to AF Group Srl. Claudio has always given the imprint to the sound of Audio Analogue and his goal with Airtech is to create a signature to use on different types of high-end products...

  • Our products
    • AUDIO ANALOGUE - ABsolute designed by AIRTECH

      ABsolute is the reference line of Audio Analogue. Developed in the Airtech laboratories is at the top of the range of products, with unique and no compromise solutions.

    • AUDIO ANALOGUE - Anniversary designed by AIRTECH

      The Anniversary line incorporates the concepts and references of our brand historical devices in a more modern way and developed with the technology of Airtech laboratories.

    • AUDIO ANALOGUE - PureAA

      A simple design together with the reference sound performance to create products with the best value for money on the market.

    • Airtech - Cables

      A new line of uncompromising audio cables designed to get the best performance from your audio system.

    • Airtech - Loudspeakers

      A speaker unique in the World that combines magneto-planar and dynamic technologies.
